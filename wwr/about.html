History

Founded in 1998, Wild River Expeditions started as a small group of adventure enthusiasts who wanted to share their passion for white water rafting with the world. Our journey began on the rugged rivers of Colorado, where our team of expert guides led small groups through some of the most challenging and breathtaking rapids in the region.

Over the years, we have grown into a renowned rafting company known for our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and unforgettable adventure experiences. Today, Wild River Expeditions operates on multiple rivers across the country, offering a range of trips from family-friendly excursions to adrenaline-pumping challenges for seasoned rafters. Join us and become part of our story!
