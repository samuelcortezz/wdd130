Overview

Purpose

As the owner of a rafting business, the purpose of establishing an online presence is to effectively reach potential customers and provide them with information about our rafting adventures. By having a website, we aim to showcase the thrilling experiences we offer, highlight safety measures, and facilitate booking inquiries. Through our online platform, we seek to promote our brand and attract adventure enthusiasts who are seeking memorable rafting experiences.

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website consists of adventure seekers, outdoor enthusiasts, and families looking for thrilling recreational activities.

  • Avid adventurers seeking adrenaline-pumping experiences in the great outdoors.
  • Families planning adventurous vacations with children of various ages.
  • Corporate groups or teams interested in team-building exercises and outdoor adventures.

To ensure our website effectively caters to their needs, we need to consider the following scenario questions:

  1. What age groups are most likely to be interested in our rafting adventures?
  2. Our target audience includes individuals of various age groups, from young adults to middle-aged adventure enthusiasts. Families with children are also a significant demographic.
  3. What level of experience do our potential customers have with rafting?
  4. We welcome individuals with varying levels of experience, from beginners looking for introductory rafting trips to seasoned rafters seeking challenging whitewater experiences.
  5. What are the key factors influencing their decision to book a rafting trip?
  6. Factors such as safety, adventure level, trip duration, and the overall experience play a crucial role in their decision-making process.
